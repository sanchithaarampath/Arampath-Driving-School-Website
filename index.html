Driving Excellence Since 2000

Arampath Driving School has been shaping confident, safe drivers for over 26 years. Founded with a commitment to road safety and skill-based training, we've grown into one of Sri Lanka's most respected driving schools.

We operate 3 branches: Narammala, Giriulla, and our Dambadeniya training center — all staffed with well trained qualified instructors dedicated to your success behind the wheel.

Our Vision

What drives us forward every single day.

"දක්ෂ විනයගරුක රියදුරන් බිහිකිරීම අපගේ පරමාර්ථයයි!"

"Our goal is to produce competent and discipline drivers."

"திறமையான ஒழுக்கத்துடன் கூடிய ஓட்டுநர்களை உருவாக்குவது எங்கள் முக்கிய குறிக்கோளாகும்!"

At Arampath Driving School, we believe every driver has the potential to be safe, skilled, and responsible on the road. Our vision is to make Sri Lanka's roads safer — one driver at a time.

Competence

Training drivers with the technical skills, knowledge, and confidence to handle any situation on the road safely.

Discipline

Instilling respect for traffic laws, fellow road users, and the responsibility that comes with being behind the wheel.

Safer Roads

Contributing to a safer Sri Lanka by producing graduates who are not just licensed — but truly road-ready drivers.
